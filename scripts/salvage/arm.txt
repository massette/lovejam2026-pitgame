=title SALVAGE MANUAL  /   Robot Longarm
=health 5
=image salvage/arm
A robot arm with far too many joints.
A distinct characteristic of the second age of robotics.

=font bold
[BREAKDOWN INSTRUCTIONS]
Due to the size of the arm, this can be a tedious breakdown.

=begin
Starting from the shoulder, remove the first elbow joint.
4 alloy may be reclaimed.
=await ALLOY 4
=next

Remove the extensor.
1 polymer may be reclaimed.
=await POLYMER 1

Remove the upper segment of the arm, which is now disconnected.
8 alloy may be reclaimed.
=await ALLOY 8
=next

Repeat for all remaining joints.
=await ALLOY 4
=next
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 8
=next
=await ALLOY 4
=next
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 8
=next
=await ALLOY 4
=next
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 8
=next
=end

=begin
Finally, the hand may be removed and disassembled.
See section "Robot Hand".
=await ALLOY 6
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 14
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 14
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 14
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 14
=await POLYMER 1
=await ALLOY 14
=next
=end
